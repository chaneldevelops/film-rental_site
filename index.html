About us

Scene Setters is a film equipment rental company that is dedicated to helping filmmakers, photographers, and event professionals bring their creative vision to life. Our team of experienced technicians and creatives understands the importance of having the right equipment to succeed. We offer a wide range of equipment including cameras, lighting, lenses, sound equipment, props, and camera accessories to fit your project's specific needs.

Our rental packages are customizable to ensure that you get the equipment and support you need to achieve your goals. We have proudly helped our clients succeed in a variety of projects, from short films and commercials to documentaries and live events with 10 years of experience in the industry. Our clients have trusted us to provide high-quality gear and exceptional customer service, allowing them to focus on creating.
